What is MoTrip

MoTrip is an application in which we provide services for people who want to split travel cost with others for cheaper cost and connect them to those who want to earn side income as independent travel guide. MoTrip also a platform to connect people with new friends and experience priceless moments together!

Who is MoTrip

MoTrip is an Indonesian technology company with a mission to provide wide range of services in travel
industry with the use of sharing economy concept, enabling people to create a more cost efficient
travel with countless amazing travel itineraries from numerous independent travel guides. MoTrip committed to be your fun traveling partner by showing people various unique travel
destination and travel itineraries while creating wonderful memories with others.

Why is MoTrip

Best & Honest Price

Get the most competitive price for your travel through the split cost tour feature and offers from our extensive network of independent tour guide with best price guarantee.
There are no hidden charges in MoTrip, we offer the most honest price available.


Easy and Secure Payment

Our in app wallet ensures your money gets to the tour creator—that’s why we ask you to always pay through MoTrip and never wire money or pay someone directly. We also offer various payment options such as ATM Transfer, Credit Card, and Internet Banking. To ensure your money is safe, MoTrip required the tour creator to upload the invoices, and share the tickets that has been bought and paid. Tour members also could request the proofs and billings of the funds used to the tour creator.

Real & Trustable Reviews

MoTrip only shows the real and genuine reviews from our users from various countries. After each trip completed, every trip members including the tour creator is required to give reviews about the trip, the members, the tour creator, and then give feedback to MoTrip. We appreciate each reviews and feedbacks.


Trust and Safety

Your safety is our top priority. Your trust is very important to us and we try to meet your trust with various safety precautions. We will do an extensive background checks to ensure that none our customers is a criminals and we also evaluates every activity to ensures there’s no suspicious activities that could endanger our members’ safety.


Account & Profile Protection

We also develop a facial recognition feature to protect our members profile & account. When you log in from other device members are required multi-factor authentication and sending your account alerts. Other members only could see your basic profile such as name, hobby, profile photo, and followers.


Secure Messaging

Our in app messaging feature is very secure with encryption, through our messaging feature members and tour creator could chat each other to discuss various things and coordinate everything.


Emergency Contact & Panic Button

When you first filling your account profile, we always required you to enter your emergency contact, so we could immediately contact them should anything happens. We also linked your live location through GPS to nearest police station and hospital. We also will immediately notify nearest other MoTrip members and police in your location when you hit our panic button.
